# GERMES Hexagon + Star Gate Mapping

## Geometric Root

The GERMES hexagon is the projection of a cube viewed along one of its space diagonals.

- 8 vertices of the cube = 8 states of Q³
- 2 poles (`---` and `+++`) project into the **center**
- Remaining 6 vertices form the regular hexagon

## Hexagon with Symbols

```text
1. --+   молния быстрая        →
2. -+-   глина горячая         ◇
3. +--   дым тёплый            △
4. -++   ветер порывистый      ▲
5. +-+   туман прохладный      □̸
6. ++-   течение медленное     —

Center: --- ↔ +++     marked as ✕
```

## Antipodes (red diameters through center)

```text
--+  ↔  -++      (1 ↔ 4)
-+-  ↔  +-+      (2 ↔ 5)
+--  ↔  ++-      (3 ↔ 6)
```

These are natural places for the conflict symbol `✕`.

## Two Triangles (Star of David)

**Green triangle (weight 1)** — awakening:
```text
--+ ,  -+- ,  +--
symbols: →  ◇  △
```

**Yellow triangle (weight 2)** — consolidation:
```text
-++ ,  +-+ ,  ++-
symbols: ▲  □̸  —
```

## How to use together

- GERMES gives **topology** (where states sit relative to each other)
- Star Gate gives **character** of the relation (how they are connected)

Together they form a two-level notation:
1. Position in the hexagon
2. Type of edge between positions
